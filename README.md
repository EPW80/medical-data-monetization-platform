# Product Description: Secure Medical Data Marketplace

Our platform is a blockchain-based marketplace that connects individuals who want to monetize their medical data with researchers who need access to health records. Key features include:

## 1. For Individuals

- Securely upload personal medical data
- Maintain control over data sharing
- Earn money by selling anonymized health information

## 2. For Researchers

- Purchase anonymized health records
- Filter and search for relevant data
- Access valuable information for medical research

## 3. Privacy and Security

- Data encryption to protect sensitive information
- Smart contracts to manage ownership and transactions
- Anonymization to prevent exposure of full medical histories

## 4. Focus

- Strictly a data marketplace for monetization and research
- Not a social network or health management tool

This platform aims to create a mutually beneficial ecosystem where individuals can profit from their medical data while researchers gain access to valuable health information, all within a secure and privacy-focused environment.
